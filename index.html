FAQ

how does it work?

Simply place your order online, customize your pizza, and choose delivery or pickup. We prepare it fresh, and in just 30 minutes, it’s ready for you to enjoy!

how long does it take for delivery?

Our delivery typically takes just 30 minutes, ensuring your pizza arrives hot and fresh.

can I order for huge parties?

Yes, absolutely! We offer large orders for parties and events. Just let us know in advance, and we’ll make sure everything is prepared fresh and delivered on time.

how much protein it contains?

The protein content in each pizza depends on your chosen toppings and crust. Check our menu’s nutritional info for exact protein details per slice!

is it cooked with oil?

Yes, we use a light amount of oil in preparing the dough and in cooking some toppings to enhance flavor. Let us know if you prefer any modifications!
